How Rohan Cracked His First Hackathon with NEXTEP

5 min read â€¢ Published: June 15, 2023

Rohan Sharma

NEXTEP Student Ambassador

Three months ago, I had never written a line of code. Today, I'm holding a trophy from my first hackathon win. This is the story of how NEXTEP helped me bridge that gap.

The Beginning

Like many students, I was fascinated by technology but didn't know where to start. The computer science syllabus at my college felt outdated, and online resources were overwhelming. That's when I discovered NEXTEP's structured learning paths.

The Turning Point

NEXTEP's "30 Days to Code" challenge gave me the confidence to start building. Their mentor support was crucial - whenever I got stuck, there was someone to guide me within hours.

"The hackathon wasn't about winning for me - it was about proving to myself that I could build something real. NEXTEP gave me the tools and community to make that happen."

The Hackathon Experience

With NEXTEP's hackathon preparation workshops, I learned how to approach problems strategically. Their team matching system connected me with two other beginners, and together we built an app that helps farmers detect crop diseases using AI.

What's Next?

This is just the beginning. I'm now mentoring other students through NEXTEP's peer learning program, paying forward the support I received.
