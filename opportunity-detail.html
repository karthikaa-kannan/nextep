About the Hackathon

The Global Student Hackathon is an annual 48-hour coding competition that brings together talented students from around the world to solve real-world problems through technology.

This year's theme is "Tech for Good" - we're challenging participants to build solutions that address pressing global issues in education, healthcare, sustainability, and more.

With over $10,000 in prizes and mentorship from industry leaders, this is your chance to showcase your skills, learn from experts, and connect with like-minded peers.

Eligibility

  • Open to all students currently enrolled in a university or college
  • Recent graduates (within 1 year) are also eligible
  • Teams of 2-4 participants (can be from different institutions)
  • All skill levels welcome - from beginners to advanced coders
